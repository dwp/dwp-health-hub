    • Infrastructure Architecture

  • Simple Queuing Service and Simple Notification Service to replace Rabbit MQ federated broker pattern currently being worked through by Narender and Chris Hulley. This will improve the reliability and maintenance involved in managing health messaging/events
  • Elastic Container Service (ECS) proof of concept being worked through by Lewis and Narender who is pulling to together a Design Authority submission. This will replace single micro service to single host architecture and have huge benefits in terms of provisioning, deployments and costs
