Access to work: New realeased

Home

The Access to Work team are pleased to inform that today (16th January 2019), the renewal application service on gov.uk successfully went live in private beta.

The digital team, colleagues in operations and OED have worked continuously together to create this service, and their passion and commitment for the Access to Work service has shone through. My thanks to all involved.

However, before we can start to inform customers that they can apply to renew their grant online, we need sign off to use a data extract that helps us to identify the correct customers. This issue has now been escalated to our Deputy Director James Parker who is liaising with the SRO for sign off.

In the next day or two we hope to send you another update to advise that we have sent our first batch of renewal reminders, and subsequently that customers are using the renewal online application.
