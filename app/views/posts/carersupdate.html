Carer's ALlowance: Update

Home

The Carers Allowance Digital Service, which was the first DWP Digital service launched in 2014 has been successfully moved from UKCloud hosting to Amazon Web Service (AWS), to meet the strategic direction of DWP Technology Services and to bring alignment of Care with other Digital services.

The move entailed converting the entire Citizen facing application to a DWP approved framework whilst retaining the same, look and feel meeting the latest GDS standards; and rewriting the entire backend Microservice structure to meet current standards reusing common Digital (CHAPS specific) components which had not been available when the service was originally built. The deployment was fully automated, and now provides enhanced resilience with failover to other hosting sites as well as automated rebuild in the event of loss of service.

The new service was launched on 17th December, with deployment spread over three days to gradually ramp up the service to full load. The transition was seamless to citizens with zero down time and had no impact on day to day operational activities.

Customer completion times for Care applications are being closely monitored, with a further Release planned on 15th January to resolve the minor bugs identified along with decommissioning the UKCloud hosting to reduce costs.
