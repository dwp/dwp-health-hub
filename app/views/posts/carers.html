Carer's Allowance Digital Service

Home

Apply and Maintain Carer's Allowance is an agile project that aims to build out from the citizen service, to replace the current legacy system (CACS) Initaially, this system will deal with the 'simple' Carer's Allowance cases, ingesting the information from teh citizen and presenting it to the agent for verificiation and decision. We will then iterate to include the more complex cases; integrating with CIS and Qualifying Benefit systems; enhancing the citizen service to include document upload and a save function; and exploring automating decisions.
