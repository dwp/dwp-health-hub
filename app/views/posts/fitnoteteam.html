Send your fit note

Home

A digital service that allows ESA claimants to send a digital image of their fit note to the department using a mobile phone or other device.

Claimants need to provide regular proof of illness/disability to maintain payment of their ESA until they have been fully assessed- this period is usually 13 weeks but can last longer.

Often fit notes are lost in transit or the process can take up to 15 days which results in claimants either coming in to jobcentres or sending duplicates (which is additional work for the doctor). The digital service sends the fit note directly into DRS reducing the time lag to a matter of minutes.

This has already reduced footfall in jobcentres and also reduced the number of duplicate fit notes being sent. For userswithout access to mobile devices the service also operates on a desktop/laptop where users can upload an image- this is especially useful for customers who access digital services through libraries and other partners.
