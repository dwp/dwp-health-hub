Improving the journey for New Style Employment & Support Allowance (ESA) customers

Home

From December 2018, all ESA Income Related new claims will have migrated to Universal Credit (UC) leaving approximately 240k new ESA Contributory (New Style ESA) claims per annum subject to current ESA benefit rules and maintained in JSAPS.

The current journey for our customers is very clunky and involves a number of clerical processes. There can also be confusion when contacting the departmentsand and the customer is subject to a number of handoffs. This can lead to a number of issues such as the customer not receiving the benefit they are entitled to.

The team, headed up by Catherine Buchanan in Leeds, is working to provide a solution within Health by breathing life into Apply for ESA online service. They plan to provide a new front end New Style ESA service online that can be utilised by UC whilst integrating seamlessley with 'back of house' systems using intelligent Automation to ingest the claim data into the appropriate system (CAM or JSAPS) without call agent intervention.

They will also be working alongside UC colleagues to develop an appropriate claims adjudication process for New Style ESA online and improve direction for users.
