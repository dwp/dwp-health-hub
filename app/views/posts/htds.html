Health Transformation Digital Service: Update

By Emma Anderton Associate Product Owner

Home

We are now in week 14 of private beta, handling Universal Credit Full Service work capability assessments in DWP led Assessment Centres (Tresco House, London & Five Ways House, Birmingham).

Key data so far:

  • 81 new claim referrals have been made onto the service
  • 6 cases to date have progressed to having an appointment for a face to face assessment being booked
  • 2 of the customers booked have subsequently attended their assessment and had decisions made on their case
  • Given the limited MVP scope, we anticipated referring back a number of cases to BAU processing however the numbers have been higher than expected. This is helping to prioritise our backlog with new functionality being developed to improve the retention rate on the new service

    • The teams are iterating and building out extended functionality with continuous releases of software including:

  • Editing customer details
  • Editing customer’s GP details
  • Adding other healthcare professional details
  • Adding events to the timeline
  • Recording additional illnesses and disabilities
  • Recording the customer arrival time for their appointment
  • Launching the assessment service upon customer arrival

    • Future designs are being tested through user research. In addition to our regular research at DWP Assessment Centres, we are now undertaking research with Centre for Health and Disability Assessment’s (CHDA) supplier colleagues. To date this has covered seven different locations across a broad range of job roles and experience levels.
