Xera

Home

Xera is the break down of our technology into components for re-use across Health, CHAPS and wider DWP.

We will provide an adaptable and extensive component architecture that removes reliance on previous legacy products where change can often be complex and lengthy.

Xera is defined by categorising the work we do into Services, Products, Components, Patterns and Tools.

Each of these components are independently deployable, scalable and based around business capabilities.

Our vision

• To get the right support to the right people when they need it

• To reduce the burden on our users through the direct sharing and re-use of data

• Reduce complexity, increase transparency and build trust in our relationships with customers

• To deliver services that provide tailored journeys depending on your condition and circumstance

• To support our people with services that improve accuracy and reduce the number of successful appeals
