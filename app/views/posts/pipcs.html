We had two recent PIPCS releases, which have included important upgrades to both maintain the support of the PIPCS system and a number of system fixes/changes identified by both Technicians and Users that needed to be deployed to live.

PIP CS R8.0 deployed to live successfully on 07/07/18

Teh Curam product upgrade included in R8.0 ensures the core product on which PIPCS is built continues to be supported by the product vendor IBM, whilst at the same time improved the look and feel of some of the PIPCS secreens, although this will take time for users to get used to initial feedback has been positive. It also included 3 urgent problem fixes for the business. These were; PRB4247312 WS1-36 Consider contact task created irrespective of the contact being cancelled PRB4247829 TASK WSHS_34 DLO Status was being generated when teh address is no longer DWPlogo PRB4248957 Primary action link on Task038 produces an error

We also provided a problem fix in PIPCS R8.0.1 on the 13/07 which has adjusted PIPCS Launch process for Serco Camlite users and some users in Warbreck House, meaning that the performance of that launch for the majority of users is a lot quicker than before, though we are aware that for some users the improvements seen is still not satisfactory, which continues to be investigated.

We wouldn't normally deploy major releases so close together, however the business were keen that the changes included in R9.0 didn't slip, so the project made special efforts to ensure the two major releases were depolyed only two weeks apart

PIP CS R9.0 deployed to live successfully on 21/07/18

This included;

- 13 business enhancements/improvements, such as GDPR Mota Mailshot, PIP Organisation Structure change and Change to Descriptors in Decision Assist

- 21 problem fixes which represent 45% of the outstanding stock of problems on PIPCS and as a result, we expect to see a decrease in the numbers of incidents raised against PIPCS.

Monitiring of all the changes continues to be monitored and any issues raised addressed asap.
